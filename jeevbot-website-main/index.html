The Stody Behind

Indian Livestock Sector is facing limitations in disease reporting, proper networking and communication. In a nutshell, India lacks a robust system ICT communication and networking system for Livestock Owner, Veterinarians, Livestock Assistants and many more stake holders related to the sector.

Nandi is a multi-functionality conversational bot developed by us. It is neither a website, nor a mobile application. Nandi is a Whatsapp Bot. And this is the reason it is the easiest solution ever developed for this sector.

Nandi will support realtime disease reporting by Animal Farmer which will be verified by backend veterinary team. Later this data will be trained on a Convolutional Neural Network which will enable Auto Identification of diseases, disease forecasting and surveillance. The data will be shared with government as well.

This will solve the issues of under reporting. But, that's not the end. Nandi has much more to offer. It has all the information needed for an Animal Farmers. It has first ever 24*7 veterinary hotline in India. It has realtime animal reporting with support to add images and videos with quick action over them.

Nandi is a transformation to the Livestock and Veterinary sector of India. It has many more features that are explained in the presentation attached with short term attainable future goals as well.
